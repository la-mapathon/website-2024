Day 3 Schedule

International Meetup

Our final day will be primarily virtual, so we hope you can attend in real-time!

Events will include:

  • Lightning presentations summarizing events from each region.
  • International Breakout Rooms - Meet other mappers from across the globe, share the maps you've created and share your experience participating in the Mapathon!
  • Vote for your favorite maps on Padlet!
  • Awards! We'll conclude with the results from our UN Smart Mapping panel of judges, as well as a special People's Choice Award.
